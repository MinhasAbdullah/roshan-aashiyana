Our Story

Built for Pakistan's
Real Estate Market

Roshan Aashiyana was born out of a simple observation — finding a home in Pakistan was unnecessarily complex, opaque and stressful. We set out to change that.

Our platform brings buyers, sellers, renters and verified dealers onto one trusted space, with transparent pricing, verified listings and a smooth experience from search to deal.
